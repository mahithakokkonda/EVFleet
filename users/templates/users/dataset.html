Dataset Overview

Unique ID: Unique identifier for each entry in the dataset.
Username: Name of the user associated with the vehicle (e.g., driver or fleet manager).
Email: Email address of the user.
Role: The role of the user, such as driver or fleet manager.
Make: The manufacturer or brand of the electric vehicle.
Acceleration (0-100 km/h): Time taken by the vehicle to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h, measured in seconds.
Top Speed: Maximum speed the vehicle can achieve, measured in km/h.
Electric Range: Distance the vehicle can travel on a full charge, measured in kilometers.
Total Power: Total power output of the vehicle, measured in kilowatts (kW).
Length: Length of the vehicle, measured in millimeters (mm).
Width: Width of the vehicle, measured in millimeters (mm).
Cargo Volume: Total cargo capacity of the vehicle, measured in liters.
Height: Height of the vehicle, measured in millimeters (mm).
Total Torque: Total torque generated by the vehicle, measured in Newton-meters (Nm).
Wheelbase: Distance between the front and rear axles of the vehicle, measured in millimeters (mm).
GVWR: Gross Vehicle Weight Rating, the maximum allowable weight of the vehicle, including passengers and cargo, measured in kilograms (kg).
Seats: Number of seats available in the vehicle.
Maintenance Cost: Cost incurred for maintaining the vehicle, measured in the local currency.
Max. Payload: Maximum weight the vehicle can carry, measured in kilograms (kg).
Battery Capacity: Capacity of the battery, measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh).
Pincode: Postal code indicating the location associated with the entry.
